### Hero Section Audit
- **Headline:** "Turn verbal agreements into signed receipts — in 90 seconds."
  - Formula: Problem-Agitation-Solution + speed promise
  - Research grade: **B+** — Clear value prop, but "signed receipts" may confuse (legal term vs product name)
  - A/B test candidate: "Stop scope creep before it starts — send binding agreement receipts in 90 seconds"
- **CTA Primary:** "Send your first receipt — free" → `/demo`
  - Action-oriented copy. Good.
- **CTA Secondary:** "See pricing" → `/pricing`
  - Evaluation-stage funnel step. Proper.
- **Stats:** "90s To send", "2.3× Faster approvals", "−41% Fewer disputes"
  - Claims not sourced/supported. Need footnotes or link to methodology.
- **Social Proof Bar:** "Trusted by 200+ agencies" + 4 fake logos
  - **Critical Issue:** This is false social proof. Must be removed or replaced with "Join the early access list" before launch. Regulatory risk in SA (ASA guidelines on misleading advertising).

### CTA Section Audit
- **Headline:** "Stop losing money to scope creep."
  - Strong pain-point headline.
- **CTA Primary:** "Start free" → `/demo`
- **CTA Secondary:** "Book a 15-min demo" → `/demo`
  - **Issue:** Both CTAs route to the same destination. Misleading. If there is no demo booking flow, re-label to "Watch a 2-min demo" or create actual booking page.
- **Trust badges:** "SOC 2 Type II in progress", "99.9% uptime", "End-to-end encrypted"
  - Good compliance signaling.

### Pricing Page Audit
- **Pages present:** `/pricing` (dedicated page) + PricingSection on homepage
- **Annual Toggle:** Present ✅ — follows best practice (per Argos research: annual default improves LTV 10–15%)

- **4-tier structure:** Free / Starter ($19) / Pro ($39, "Best value") / Enterprise ($149)
  - Per research, 3-tier anchors middle as "safe" choice. 4-tier may cause decision paralysis.
  - **Recommendation:** Test collapsing Starter+Pro into one mid-tier or clearly differentiate value.
- **"Best value" badge:** On Pro. Good visual anchor.
- **Comparison grid:** Present. Clear checkmarks. Good for evaluation-stage users.
- **CTAs per tier:**
