## Quote Card Text

> "A verbal yes is not a commitment. It is intent. And intent fades."

## Caption

The most expensive word in sales is "yes." Especially when it is spoken, not signed.

Top-performing teams know this. They do not wait for the contract. They confirm the deal terms immediately after the call with a conversation receipt.

Three things happen when you do this:

1. Ghosting drops. Prospects who sign a summary are harder to ignore.
2. Contracts move faster. Legal already knows the terms are approved.
3. Reps are protected if the deal changes later.

The best teams close faster not because they are more persuasive.
They are better at documenting the yes.
