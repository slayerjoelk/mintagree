## Quote Card Text

> "Every agency has a story like this. The ones that stop repeating it are the ones that document before they start."

## Caption

Scope creep does not announce itself. It shows up as a "small tweak" and leaves as a $12,000 invoice dispute.

The most expensive part of client work is not the labor. It is the disagreement about what was agreed.

Three ways to protect yourself before the next project starts:

1. Confirm scope in writing — not in a follow-up email three days later. Immediately.

2. Get a one-time code confirmation — it takes 30 seconds and creates a binding record.

3. Reference the original thread — whether it was WhatsApp, Slack, or a call.

The agencies that grow are not the ones with the best designers. They are the ones with the cleanest documentation.

Save this for your next kickoff.
