## Twitter Thread — client-approval-workflow

1/ The average design revision generates 47 unread messages across six people. Nobody knows which version is approved. Nobody knows who has final say. This is the client approval workflow problem. And it costs agencies 8 hours a week.

2/ A functional approval workflow has four parts: clear ownership, single-version truth, structured feedback, and documented sign-off. Each one removes a specific failure point that stalls projects.

3/ Real example: a freelance designer in Cape Town replaced her email approval chain with a three-step process. Average approval cycle dropped from five days to under 24 hours. She also eliminated three bonus revision rounds per project.

Every project you do not fix is silently taxing your margin.
